Earn Ethereum Farming Hipster!

Ether Hipster Farm is the #1 hipster farming simulator and idle game on the blockchain. The more hipster you have, the more beards they lay (each hipster lays at a rate of 1 per day). Hatch more hipster with your beards to multiply your production, or cash them out for Ethereum!

Powered by Metamask

This game requires Metamask

Hipsters Are Frail Technology

To prevent inflation, Hipster Farms have several tricks over their competitors. Hipsters are attached to their progeny and one third of your nest will die if you sell your beards. Those willing to make the greatest sacrifice can become the razor, and collect tribute on every beard sale.
